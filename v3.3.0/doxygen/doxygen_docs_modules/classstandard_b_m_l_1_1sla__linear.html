Detailed Description

Calculates the specific leaf area as a linear function of thermal time.

As plants age, they tend to produce thicker leaves. Thus, the leaf mass per area increases, while its inverse, the specific leaf area, decreases.

Here, this process is modeled as a linear dependence of specific leaf area (Sp) on thermal time (TTc):

‍Sp = iSp - Sp_thermal_time_decay * TTc,

where iSp is the initial value of the specific leaf area and Sp_thermal_time_decay is the slope.

For an alternate way to model specific leaf area, see the sla_logistic module.
