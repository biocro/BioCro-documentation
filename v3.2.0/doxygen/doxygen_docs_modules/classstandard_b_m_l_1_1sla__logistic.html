Detailed Description

Calculates the specific leaf area as a logistic function of thermal time.

As plants age, they tend to produce thicker leaves. Thus, the leaf mass per area increases, while its inverse, the specific leaf area, decreases.

Here, this process is modeled as a logistic dependence of specific leaf area (Sp) on thermal time (TTc):

‍Sp = Sp_initial + (Sp_final - Sp_initial) / (1.0 + exp(-Sp_k * (TTc - Sp_TTc_0)),

where Sp_initial and Sp_final are the initial and final values of specific leaf area, respectively, Sp_k sets the width of the transition from Sp_initial to Sp_final, and Sp_TTc_0 sets the midpoint of the transition.

For an alternate way to model specific leaf area, see the sla_linear module.
